Fill out the form below to request support for Microsoft Copilot features.

This service includes two types of Copilot designed to support individual productivity for information workers.

Important exclusions:

  • This service does not include Copilot Studio (used for building custom agents or integrations)
  • Copilot licences cannot be assigned to shared mailboxes, generic accounts, or service accounts

Copilot (Web-based, Free)

  • Accessed via copilot.microsoft.com or the Copilot icon in Edge
  • Free to use
  • Recommended: Sign in with your University account for enhanced protection and data handling
  • Does not access your Microsoft 365 emails, files, or meetings

Microsoft 365 Copilot (Paid)

  • Integrated into Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, and Teams
  • Requires a paid user licence
  • Can access your University Microsoft 365 data (e.g. emails, files, calendar, chats) to assist with drafting, summarising, and analysis

Please use the dropdown to select the type of Copilot feature you require.
